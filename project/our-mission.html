Our Mission

Every corner of CSU

InterVarsity wants to reach every corner of CSU. We believe that everyone should have the chance to experience Jesus Christ personally, regardless of their backgrounds. By surrendering our lives to Jesus, we have a goal to bring service and community to CSU.

God's Justice

In our scripture studies this semester, we are focusing on God's Justice. We believe that God has made His views on Justice clear through His Word. Although these topics aren't the easiest to talk about, we believe that God has powerful things to say about justice. It's relevant in your personal life, our current culture, and on CSU's campus. We'll be investigating both the Old and New Testament to seek and find God this semester!
