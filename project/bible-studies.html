Bible Studies

InterVarsity hosts our Manuscripting Bible Studies all across CSU's campus!

You can find a Bible Study that fits your schedule! Our Bible Studies are open to students of all school years and backgrounds! If you're an experienced Christian, a new Christian, or someone just exploring religion, you are welcome with open arms!

Manuscripting the Bible

We study the Word of God through what we call manuscripting. Manuscripting is a process of communal discovery. We look at a piece of scripture with “fresh” eyes. We immerse ourselves in that time frame, and look at the scripture as if it’s the first time we’ve seen it. We ask questions together and share observations we find. Our goal is to have everyone bring out their individual ideas and perspectives, and to understand Jesus and the Bible in new ways.

Come join our community! We bring the scripture, you bring yourself!
