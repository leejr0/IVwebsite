About InterVarsity

InterVarsity Christian Fellowship is non-denominational, inter-denominational organization based on campuses around the United States. InterVarsity focuses on spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ to undergraduate students, graduate students, and campus faculty. We spread the word of Jesus Christ through Evangelism, Discipleship, and Service. Through our acts of kindness and Bible Studies, we hope and pray that Jesus would make an impact on the lives of all people on campuses around the country.

InterVarsity finds valuable community in its diversity. We are open and welcoming to people of different backgrounds and beliefs, and we look forward to different perspectives of God’s Word, whether from a new Christian, a Christian with years of experience, or someone exploring God’s Word for the first time.
